Summer Population Exposure to Urban Surface Heat and Its Drivers in London and Birmingham: A Comparative MSOA-Level Analysis

Powell A. Ndlovu · MSc GIS & Climate Change · Swansea University
↗ Explore the Heat Exposure Dashboard

Abstract. This dashboard reveals how heatwave land surface temperature and P90-defined population, elderly and deprivation exposure cluster across London and Birmingham MSOAs. Local Moran's I distinguishes significant High–High and Low–Low clusters from spatial outliers, turning city-wide heat patterns into neighbourhood-level evidence for targeted, equitable heat adaptation.
